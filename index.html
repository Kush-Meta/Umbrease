What We Are

Umbrease is an urban umbrella rental system designed at Emory to make staying dry simple. Whether you're on the go or caught off guard, we've got you covered — literally. We’re proud to be incubated by forward-thinking startup accelerators helping us refine and scale this vision. We're a team of undergraduate students from Emory University trying to make our campus more sustainable - one rainy day at a time.
